Risotto

Main Page

Risotto is an italian dish made of rice, creamy sauce and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Shallot
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Rice
  • Butter
  • Mushrooms

Steps

  1. Warm the chicken in a pan at medium heat and cover it.
  2. Heat the olive oil in another pan until shimmering. Then add the shallot, garlic, mushrooms and some spices.
  3. Add the rice and toast.
  4. Add the butter.
  5. Put everything in the same pan and mix.
  6. Let the dish rest for 15 minutes.
