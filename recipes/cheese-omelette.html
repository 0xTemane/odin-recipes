Cheese Omelette

Main Page

Cheese Omelette is a succulent dish that you can make with eggs and cheese. It's very customizable

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Break the eggs in a bowl.
  2. Mix the eggs.
  3. Put the eggs in a hot pan with a bit of oil/butter.
  4. Let it cook for 2 minutes and mix sometimes.
  5. Add Cheese on the omelette.
  6. Cover the pan for 30 seconds.
  7. Put the cheese omelette in a plate and let it rest for 5 minutes!
