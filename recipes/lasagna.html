Lasagna

Main Page

Lasagna is an Italian pasta dish made from wide, flat sheets layered with tomato or white sauce, cheese, and often meat or vegetables, then baked until bubbling and golden.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Spices/Seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the Ricotta texture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to YOU.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
